Wealth Protection

Wealth Protection is about helping families in Canada understand the risks that can quietly damage their hard-earned wealth — from debt exposure and insurance gaps to fraud, poor documentation, medical emergencies, and estate confusion.

The goal is simple: translate these risks in plain language and build practical readiness tools that help families protect their financial foundation before a crisis exposes the gaps.
